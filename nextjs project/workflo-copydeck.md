# Workflo.it Bilingual Copy Deck

This copy deck provides draft copy in both **English (EN)** and **Dutch (NL)** for every customer-facing page that will be rewritten during the Workflo.it website relaunch.  Each section contains:
• Page Purpose  
• SEO Meta Title + Description  
• Hero Heading & Sub-heading  
• Core Body Copy outline  
• Primary Call-to-Action (CTA)

> Feel free to adjust wording, tone or length to fit final design and brand guidelines.

### EN
**Page purpose** – Instantly communicate Workflo.it’s value proposition for Amsterdam-based SMEs and capture leads.  
**Meta title** – Workflo.it | Amsterdam’s Trusted IT Growth Partner  
**Meta description** – Stop worrying about IT problems. Workflo transforms technology into a growth engine with managed IT, cloud and cybersecurity expertise. Book a free assessment today.
